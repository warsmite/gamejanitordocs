GameJanitor

Gameserver management that gets out of your way.

Deploy, configure, and monitor gameservers from a single panel or cli. No vendor lock-in. No magic. Just a clean control plane for people who run servers.

Get Started View Source
terminal 
$ gamejanitor deploy minecraft --name minceraft
          --env EULA=true
          deploying minceraft
          memory: 4096M
          port: 25565

          ready minceraft is live
          address: 0.0.0.0:25565
          panel: http://localhost:8080/servers/mc-01

          $ gamejanitor status
          SERVER STATUS PLAYERS
          UPTIME
          minceraft live 3/20 2d 14h
          valheim-prod live 1/10 5d 03h
          palworld-test idle 0/16 0d 08h

One Command Deploy

Pick a game, set your config, deploy. No Dockerfiles to write, no YAML to wrestle. Game Janitor handles it.

Multi-Game, Multi-Server

Minecraft, Valheim, Palworld, Terraria, Factorio. One panel for all of them. Add your own game definitions.

Real Monitoring

CPU, memory, player counts, crash detection. Know what's happening without SSHing into every box.

Actually Open Source

MIT licensed. No "open core" bait-and-switch. Fork it, modify it, self-host it forever. Your servers, your rules.
